About Our School

Established in 2005, Krupanidhi Christ Convent & High School has been providing quality CBSE education to students in Bangalore for nearly two decades. Our campus in Jigani provides a perfect environment for holistic development with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty.

We offer education from LKG to 10th standard with a focus on academic excellence, character formation, and extracurricular development. Learn more about our philosophy and values.

Our School Facilities

Krupanidhi Christ Convent & High School provides state-of-the-art facilities to ensure a conducive learning environment for our students.

Infrastructure

Our school boasts modern infrastructure designed to facilitate effective learning. We have spacious, well-ventilated classrooms equipped with smart boards, dedicated subject laboratories for science and computer studies, a well-stocked library, and a multipurpose auditorium for events and assemblies.

The campus includes separate play areas for different age groups, ensuring safe and appropriate recreational spaces for all students. Our infrastructure is regularly maintained and upgraded to meet the evolving needs of 21st-century education.
